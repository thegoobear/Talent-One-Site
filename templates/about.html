ABOUT US

Founded in 1998 by Anne and Rudy Greene, Talent One is a film, television, print, and industrial talent agency based in Raleigh, NC. We provide experienced performers to productions large and small nationwide, with credits ranging from independent film and industrials to national commercials and studio tentpoles. With 20 years of experience representing both SAG-AFTRA and non-union performers, you can rest assured that your career or production is in the right hands.
